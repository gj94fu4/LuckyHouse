Research Assistant in Department of Physics • September 2011 - July 2019

Loop Quantum Cosmology (LQC)
This is the theme of my doctoral research. My innovative framework enables me to probe the universe that pre-existed before the Big Bang under the context of LQC.
Array for Microwave Background Anisotropy (AMiBA)
I probed the early universe by analyzing the observational data from AMiBA. I also visited Hawaii three times, one to two months each time, for on-site operation and observation.
Edison-based Maker Project with Intel Corporation Taiwan
In this project, we developed a working product called Protison, a mobile astrophotography module, which enabled people to take astronomical pictures through a telescope by a simple web camera remotely controlled by a mobile phone.
Light-field Camera with Lite-On IT Corporation
We developed the first generation of a light-field camera which can refocus photos after they were taken. We worked on both user interface and optical experiment.

在這個專案中，我們開發了一個名為 Protison 的行動天文攝影模組，讓使用者能透過簡單的網路攝影機及手機遙控拍攝天文照片。
