AMiBA On-site Operation

Science

The purpose of AMiBA was to probe the early universe by precisely measuring the photons of cosmic microwave background (CMB) which were emitted right after the Big Bang and warmed up by galaxy clusters. The project is founded by MOST and directed by ASIAA.
